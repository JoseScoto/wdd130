Featured Trips

Don't miss out on our exciting trips. Click below to explore all our offerings!

Scenic River Drift (Beginner)

A peaceful rafting experience with calm waters, ideal for families or first-time rafters. Soak in the beauty of serene landscapes and enjoy wildlife sightings along the way. 2-3 hours, minimal rapids, perfect for relaxing and photography.

Rapids Adventure (Intermediate)

Experience the thrill of navigating class II and III rapids! This trip is designed for those seeking adventure with a moderate level of challenge. 4 hours, exciting rapids, scenic breaks for snacks and photos.

Rocky Thrill Ride (Advanced)

A high-adrenaline ride through narrow canyons and roaring class IV rapids. Perfect for experienced rafters who crave the rush of white water challenges. 6-8 hours, intense rapids, breathtaking canyon views.

Family Fun Float (Beginner-Friendly)

A relaxing day on the water for the whole family! This trip features calm currents, plenty of stops for swimming, and areas to enjoy a riverside picnic. Perfect for kids and beginners! 3 hours, gentle rapids, family-friendly activities.
